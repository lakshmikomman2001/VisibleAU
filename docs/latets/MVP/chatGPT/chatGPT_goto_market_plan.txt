# Purpose

This document defines the recommended MVP scope for VisibleAU.

Important:

This MVP is NOT:

* Phase 1 completion
* Full Phase 2 delivery
* Final product vision

This MVP is intended to:

1. Validate customer demand
2. Validate pricing
3. Validate positioning
4. Validate agency adoption
5. Generate first paying customers
6. Reduce founder risk

---

# Strategic Goal

Current Situation

VisibleAU has:

* Strong PRD
* Strong LLD
* Strong architecture
* Strong competitive research

The biggest unknown is no longer:

"Can we build it?"

The biggest unknown is:

"Will customers pay for it?"

# Final Recommendation

The objective is NOT to complete Phase 2.

The objective is:

Validate the market.

The recommended sequence is:

Build
→ Launch
→ Learn
→ Refine
→ Scale

VisibleAU should seek customer validation before further major architecture investment.

The first milestone is not enterprise adoption.

The first milestone is obtaining the first paying customers and validating that businesses will pay to understand why AI recommends competitors and how to improve their visibility.
