# Recommendation 1

Outcome Impact Layer

Priority:
HIGH

Category:
Product Positioning

Current State:

VisibleAU successfully measures:

* Visibility
* Citations
* Trust
* Competitor performance
* Prompt performance

However customers ultimately care about:

"What business impact does this create?"

Current design intentionally avoids revenue projections.

This decision should remain unchanged.

Recommendation:

Introduce:

Opportunity Impact Score

Examples:

High Impact
Medium Impact
Low Impact

Shown on:

* Recommendations
* Gap analysis
* Competitive benchmark reports
* Remediation tasks

Example:

Current:

"Add FAQ schema"

Improved:

"Add FAQ schema
Potential Impact: High
Reason: Frequently cited by AI engines in this category"

Goal:

Move conversations from metrics to outcomes.

No revenue estimates.

No fabricated forecasts.

Only evidence-based prioritization.

# Recommendation 4

AI Recommendation Explanation Standard

Priority:
HIGH

Category:
Trust

Current LLD includes ExplainabilityAnnotation.

Expand usage into a platform-wide rule.

Every recommendation should answer:

1. Where do I stand?
2. Why is this happening?
3. What should I do?
4. What impact could it have?
5. How confident is VisibleAU?

This should become a product-wide acceptance criterion.

VisibleAU should never produce unexplained recommendations.

This is a major differentiation opportunity.
