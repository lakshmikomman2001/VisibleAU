# VisibleAU Phase 2 Strategic Product Addendum v1

Version: 1.0

Status: Product Strategy Addendum

Author: ChatGPT Independent Review

Purpose:

This document supplements the existing VisibleAU Phase 2 PRD and Claude Phase 2 7-Layer LLD.

It does NOT replace the architecture.

It identifies strategic product enhancements that strengthen customer value, market differentiation, retention, explainability, and commercial outcomes.

The objective is to ensure VisibleAU evolves into an AI Visibility Operating System rather than a GEO monitoring platform.

# Strategic Product Principle

VisibleAU should help customers answer:

1. Where do I stand?
2. Why?
3. What should I do next?
4. What impact will it have?
5. How confident are we?

Every major feature should reinforce these questions.
