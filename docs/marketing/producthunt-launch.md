# VisibleAU — ProductHunt Launch Plan

## Tagline (60 chars)
AI search visibility for Australian SMBs

## Description (260 chars)
See how ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini and Perplexity describe your business. VisibleAU audits your brand's AI visibility across 4 engines, scores it 0-100, and gives actionable recommendations. Built for AU tradies, allied health and SaaS.

## Maker comment (Sri posts this)
> I built VisibleAU because I noticed Australian small businesses had no way to know if ChatGPT or Gemini even mentioned them when customers asked.
>
> Unlike Google where you can see your rankings, AI search engines are a black box. VisibleAU runs your business through 200 AI prompts across 4 engines, scores your visibility 0-100 with statistical confidence intervals, and tells you exactly what to fix.
>
> Key decisions: AU-first (not another US tool), transparent pricing in AUD, dimension-level scoring (not just "you show up or you don't"), and research-backed recommendations from Princeton GEO, Ahrefs, and SE Ranking studies.
>
> Solo founder, built over 12 weekends. Happy to answer any questions about the scoring methodology or AU market.
